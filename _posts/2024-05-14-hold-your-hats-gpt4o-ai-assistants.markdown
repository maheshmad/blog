## AI Assistants are not just glorified chat bots

We are going to be in the world of personalized AI assistants to which most of our personal and eventually professional work is going be delegated.

As a tongue and cheek example below, I was imagining a world of Insurance underwrites (because I was working for the P&C insurance domain at that time) sipping piña colada on the beach somewhere and letting their AI assistants doing all their work.

![Personalized AI Assistant chatting with each other!]({{ '/assets/images/linkedin-article-2024-05-14/image3-personalized-assistant.jpg' | relative_url }})
*Personalized AI Assistant chatting with each other!*

Similarly imagine…other use cases like:

- **Virtual Health Assistant**: Imagine having an AI assistant that not only reminds you to take your medication but also monitors your health metrics, offers dietary advice based on your medical history, and schedules appointments with healthcare providers when necessary.
- **Travel Companion**: Your AI assistant can plan your entire trip, from booking flights and hotels to suggesting sightseeing spots and restaurants based on your preferences. It can also provide real-time updates on flight delays or changes in travel plans.
- **Educational Aid**: Students can benefit from AI assistants by receiving personalized tutoring, accessing educational resources, and even getting help with homework assignments. These assistants can adapt to each student's learning pace and style, offering tailored support.
- **Financial Advisor**: AI assistants can analyze your spending habits, recommend budgeting strategies, and even invest money on your behalf based on your financial goals and risk tolerance. They can also provide real-time updates on market trends and investment opportunities.
- …. And more.

## Apps are dead! Long live Agents

Apps are dead! And probably the engineers at Apple have already started seeing the writing on the wall. The techtonic (word play intended :)) paradigm shift between an App store centric to an AI store centric ecosystem is going to follow very soon (in a matter of 2-3 years, if I had to bet).

## But something is missing or still lagging behind

The privacy and security aspect of the new GPT-4o is still something left to be desired and with current limitations of compute and energy consumption on the edge devices, those problems might not be solved any time "soon".

For AI Assistants to be truly secure and privacy conscious, it needs to be truly decentralized.

![Decentralized edge AI Assistants!]({{ '/assets/images/linkedin-article-2024-05-14/image5-decentralized-ai.jpg' | relative_url }})
*Decentralized edge AI Assistants!*

## Future: Software to AI(a)ware ecosystem

Apart from everyone retooling themselves and rewriting their Apps as "**AI Aware agents**", I believed 5 years ago (which I still do) that the important part of AI is the data and its organization. Enterprises and (even your personal health/social data) needs to be reorganized in a way that will enable the AI to perform its functions without violating the privacy.
